About Sean

Built on real experience.

Sean Zwenger has spent years helping professionals find their footing during pivotal career moments. Whether you're navigating a layoff, eyeing a promotion, or making a complete career change, Sean brings a grounded, no-nonsense approach to coaching.

His philosophy is simple: you already have what it takes. Coaching is about removing the noise so you can see it clearly and communicate it with confidence.
